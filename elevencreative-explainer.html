01 · What Is ElevenCreative

One workspace, every creative asset. No tab-switching.

ElevenCreative is ElevenLabs' end-to-end AI creative platform. It brings speech synthesis, sound effects, music generation, image creation, and video production into a single browser-based workspace. The pitch: go from a raw idea to a polished, localised ad campaign without leaving one tool.

Before ElevenCreative, producing a multilingual video ad meant juggling separate tools for voiceover, music licensing, image generation, video editing, and dubbing — each with its own exports, re-uploads, and version-control headaches. ElevenCreative collapses that into a unified pipeline where assets can flow between generation steps natively.

The Problem

Fragmented Pipelines

Creative teams use 5–8 separate platforms for voice, music, video, image, and localisation. Every export/import cycle introduces delays, version mismatches, and manual rework.

The Solution

Unified AI Workspace

ElevenCreative integrates speech (V3), music (Eleven Music), sound effects, and third-party image/video models (Veo, Sora, Kling, Wan, Seedance) in one canvas. Assets pass between steps without leaving the platform.

The Result

Idea to Campaign, Same Day

Teams can produce, iterate, and localise ad creative into 70+ languages from a single workspace — collapsing what traditionally took weeks into hours.

03 · How It Works

Five pillars, two interfaces, one workspace.

ElevenCreative organises around five core creative capabilities, accessible through two production interfaces: Studio (linear timeline editing) and Flows (node-based pipeline canvas).

Pillar 01

Speech — Eleven V3

The V3 text-to-speech model supports 70+ languages with inline audio tags for emotional direction — [whispers], [excited], [sighs]. Text-to-Dialogue mode handles multi-speaker conversations with automatic turn-taking and emotional transitions. A library of 10,000+ community voices plus Instant and Professional voice cloning.

Pillar 02

Music — Eleven Music

Launched August 2025, Eleven Music generates full tracks from text prompts — with or without vocals, in multiple languages. Outputs are commercially licensed for ads, film, TV, podcasts, social media, and games. No separate music-licensing negotiation required for standard commercial use.

Pillar 03

Sound Effects

On-demand contextual sound effect generation. Integrated into both Studio and Flows as a native node type, so SFX can be layered directly into compositions without external audio tools.

Pillar 04

Image & Video

ElevenCreative integrates third-party image and video models including Google Veo, OpenAI Sora, Kling, Wan, and Seedance — over 35 models accessible from the same canvas. No separate subscriptions or API keys needed. Lip-sync capability connects generated video with voice output.

Interface A

Studio — Timeline Editor

A traditional linear timeline for frame-by-frame editing with separate tracks for video, audio, music, and captions. Best for precise finishing: trimming, layering, and manual track adjustments on existing assets.

Interface B

Flows — Node-Based Canvas

Launched March 2026. A visual pipeline builder where you chain models together on an infinite canvas. Connect an image generator to a video animator to a TTS node to a music scorer — and re-run any single node without regenerating the full chain. Designed for batch production: swap a prompt or avatar and re-execute the entire pipeline to produce campaign variants at scale. API access for programmatic execution is planned.

04 · The Ecosystem

Three platforms, one company.

ElevenLabs restructured in early 2026 from a single product into three distinct platform families. ElevenCreative is the content-creation arm. It sits alongside ElevenAgents (conversational AI) and ElevenAPI (developer infrastructure).

Content Creation

ElevenCreative

The workspace covered in this explainer. Generate, edit, and localise speech, music, image, and video. Includes Studio (timeline), Flows (canvas), and the Iconic Voice Marketplace.

Conversational AI

ElevenAgents

Real-time voice and chat agents for customer support, sales, and interactive experiences. Powered by V3 Conversational with expressive turn-taking. Used by Deutsche Telekom, Revolut, Klarna.

Developer Platform

ElevenAPI

Programmatic access to all ElevenLabs models via REST API, Python SDK, and TypeScript SDK. Supports streaming, batch processing, SOC 2, HIPAA, GDPR compliance. Global routing reduces latency by region.

07 · Decision Guide

Is ElevenCreative right for your team?

ElevenCreative is powerful for teams producing volume creative at global scale. But it's not the right tool for every situation. Here's an honest breakdown.

✓ Use ElevenCreative when

You produce ad creative in multiple languages and need same-day turnaround on localised variants.

You want one workspace for voice, music, SFX, and video instead of managing 5+ separate tools and subscriptions.

Your workflow involves batch-producing campaign variants with systematic swaps (avatars, hooks, voices, languages) — this is exactly what Flows was built for.

You need commercially licensed AI music and don't want to negotiate separate music rights for each campaign.

✗ Skip ElevenCreative when

You need deep, frame-level video editing — Studio is competent but won't replace dedicated NLEs like Premiere Pro or DaVinci Resolve.

Your use case is purely conversational AI (phone agents, chatbots) — ElevenAgents is the right product, not ElevenCreative.

Budget is tight and volume is low. Credit-based pricing burns fast during experimentation. If you're producing one video a month, the ROI doesn't stack up.

You need enterprise-grade Professional Voice Clones on V3 — PVC optimisation is still in progress and clone quality may lag earlier models for now.

You require fully self-hosted or air-gapped deployment. ElevenCreative is cloud-only.

08 · Imbila Perspective

How we see it. What we recommend.

Our take

ElevenCreative is the strongest bet right now for teams that need to produce localised multimedia content at volume. The Flows canvas is the real differentiator — it turns ad production from a one-off creative act into a repeatable, reusable pipeline. That said, it's a credit-burning platform, and South African teams should factor in both USD-denominated pricing and the latency implications of routing through international infrastructure. ElevenLabs added global routing (auto-selecting nearest servers in the US, Netherlands, or Singapore) as default in early 2026 — that helps, but there's no African edge node yet. For teams running production-critical voice agents, latency matters more than for batch creative workflows.

Enterprise

Global Campaign Infrastructure

For enterprise marketing teams producing multilingual campaigns across markets, ElevenCreative replaces a fragmented vendor stack with a single platform. Evaluate it alongside your existing localisation partners. The SOC 2 compliance, SSO, and audit log support means it can pass most enterprise procurement reviews. Start with a Flows pilot on a single campaign before committing to platform-wide adoption.

Studio

Rapid Creative Production

Smaller creative agencies and production studios should prototype a client campaign in Flows to test the batch-variation workflow. The value proposition is strongest for performance marketing — if you're running 50+ ad variants per campaign, the per-unit cost drops significantly versus manual production. Watch credit burn rate carefully during the experimentation phase.

Dojo

Learn the AI Creative Pipeline

For teams upskilling on AI-native creative workflows, ElevenCreative is the most integrated sandbox available today. Start with Studio for basic asset generation (TTS, music, SFX), then move to Flows to understand node-based pipeline thinking. That mental model — treating creative production as reusable infrastructure rather than one-off projects — transfers to every AI creative tool.
